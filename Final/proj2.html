Pixel fighter

    As usual, no extra package used pure js+html! An interesting two player game! Fight in a web of sword and magic LOL

    Each player will control a avatar, for player1 (on the left), control keys are w,s,a,d,q and e, for player2 control key is i,k,j,l,u and o.

    Both of the avatar has the same action : slashing with sword or casting magic fire/ice ball. The players can do as much slash as they want, but since the avatars have limited magic power, the players can not cast if magic power is not enough. But no worries, the magic power will restore automaticlly.

    Game Preview.

    My favorite part of this game is, if you slash ur sword and hit opponent's fire/ice ball, the ball will become yours and will turn its head around to attack ! :))) Cheers!
